Why most AI projects stall

The problem is rarely a lack of tools. The real problem is choosing the wrong workflows, the wrong sequence, and the wrong place to start.

What usually goes wrong

  • Businesses test random AI tools with no implementation logic
  • Teams get overwhelmed by scattered experiments
  • No one knows which workflow actually matters most
  • ROI stays vague, so momentum disappears

What this assessment helps you do

  • Identify the workflows most likely to create business value
  • Prioritize opportunities by speed, practicality, and impact
  • Reduce wasted time on low-value experiments
  • Start implementation from a clearer, more strategic position
